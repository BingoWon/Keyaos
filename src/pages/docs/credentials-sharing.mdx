## Platform Fee

When your credential serves another user's request, a **6% platform fee** applies:

- **3%** from the consumer side
- **3%** from the provider side (you)

When you use your **own** credentials for your **own** requests (self-use), routing is **completely free** â€” no platform fee.

## Security

- Your API keys are encrypted at rest
- Keyaos only uses your credential to forward requests to pre-approved upstream endpoints â€” you cannot set a custom endpoint
- The platform monitors credential health and automatically stops routing to credentials that return auth errors
